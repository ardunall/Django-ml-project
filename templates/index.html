    Frequently Asked Questions

    Below are some frequently asked questions about our healthcare services.

    • Which departments have specialist doctors?

      Our hospital has specialist doctors in departments such as Gastroenterology, Orthopedics, Dermatology, Neurology, Psychiatry, Pediatrics, and many others.

    • How can I schedule an appointment?

      You can schedule an appointment online through our website or by contacting us via phone. You can also get assistance from our appointment center at the hospital.

    • What services do you offer?

      Our hospital offers a wide range of services including emergency care, surgery, intensive care, outpatient clinics, laboratory services, imaging center, and more.

    • What are your post-operative care services?

      Our post-operative care services include patient rooms, physiotherapy services, nutrition counseling, and home care services if needed.

    • Which insurance companies do you accept?

      Our hospital accepts many different insurance companies. You can check with your insurance provider to see if they cover services at our hospital.

    • How can I test for diabetes?

      You can test for diabetes by checking your blood sugar levels through various methods such as fasting blood sugar test, oral glucose tolerance test, or HbA1c test. Additionally, you can easily check if you have diabetes by logging into our website and accessing our member portal where you can find tools to assess your diabetes risk and monitor your health.
